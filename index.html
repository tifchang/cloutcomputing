How to Build Clout

Twitter analytics on driving top Tweet performance.

Introduction

How do top Tweeters gain popularity? What specifically about their posts are engaging and have contributed to their dominant Twitter following? This analysis aims to uncover the truth and reveal tactics underlying top Twitter users (i.e., users with the highest number of followers). Through this analysis, I hope to reveal patterns, similaries, and differences in their Twitter usage and content that contribute to the user's success. The revealed truths can be complemented and further understood by the user's personality, perception, and overall profile.

Data Sources

This analysis samples from the Top 10 Twitter accounts in terms of two types of accounts: pop culture figures and political figures. Using the twitteR packages, I access @justinbieber, @KimKardashian, @BarackObama, and @realDonaldTrump's posts.

From a qualitative point of view, Justin Bieber typically uses his Twitter account for music and album promotion as well as a vector for social cause advocacy. Therefore, we can see that his tweets are relatively neutral and positive when praising and commending groups for their effort related to a social cause. For example, he made numerous posts for the #MarchForOurLives campaign and commended student groups for their efforts.
